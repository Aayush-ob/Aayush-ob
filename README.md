## About

I'm a **first-author published computational researcher** in structure-based drug discovery (BTech Biotechnology). I design end-to-end *in silico* pipelines — high-throughput virtual screening, molecular docking, all-atom molecular dynamics (GROMACS), MM-PBSA/MM-GBSA binding free energy, and DFT — to discover novel inhibitors against bacterial-virulence and anticancer targets. I'm now extending into **Cloud + MLOps / AI infrastructure** to deploy and scale this kind of scientific computing.

## 🔬 Published & Ongoing Research

### Internalin H Inhibitor Discovery — *Published, first author*
> *In Silico Research in Biomedicine* (2026) · [DOI: 10.1016/j.insi.2025.100153](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.insi.2025.100153)

Anti-virulence inhibitors for *Listeria monocytogenes*. Full pipeline: HTVS → ADMET → 125 ns MD → MM/GBSA → DFT. Lead: a **Hederagenin analogue (ΔG_bind −17.09 kcal/mol)**; MD/MM-GBSA correctly re-ranked it over the top docking hit.

[![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Project-181717?style=flat&logo=github)](https://github.com/Aayush-ob/InlH-1H6u-Inhibitor-Discovery)

### GPR55 Antagonist Discovery — *Thesis defended, manuscript in preparation*
> Anticancer GPCR target (cryo-EM PDB 9IYA)

Screened **10,940 compounds across 3 binding sites**; **100 ns MD on 12 membrane-embedded complexes (~1.2 µs)**, MM-PBSA, and DFT. A novel allosteric hit outperformed the reference antagonist AM251.

[![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Project-181717?style=flat&logo=github)](https://github.com/Aayush-ob/GPR55-Antagonist-Discovery)
